How to Play

You'll be shown two real-world statistics. One value is revealed — guess whether the hidden stat is higher or lower.

  • 🟩 = Correct guess
  • 🟥 = Wrong guess
  • 10 rounds per day
  • New puzzle every midnight

Keyboard shortcuts: ↑ or H = Higher, ↓ or L = Lower, Enter = Next
